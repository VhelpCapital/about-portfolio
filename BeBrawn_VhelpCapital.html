01 — The Conviction

India Has a Protein Problem. Be Brawn Has the Answer.

At Vhelp Capital, we back founders who find elegant solutions to stubborn, structural problems. When we first encountered Be Brawn — a 330ml can of refreshing, light protein water — we weren't just impressed by the product. We were impressed by the clarity of the insight: India's protein deficiency isn't about awareness, it's about access and convenience.

Seventy-five percent of Indians are protein deficient. The existing supplements market has tried to solve this with powders, milkshakes, and bars — all of which are heavy, expensive, hard to digest, or inconvenient for daily consumption. Be Brawn's founders saw a gap that no one had filled: a light, functional, ready-to-drink protein beverage that fits seamlessly into Indian eating habits and lifestyles.

The insight was simple, the execution was not — Be Brawn built India's first protein water infused with digestive enzymes, certified across GMP, HACCP, ISO, and FDA standards, before a single major competitor had cracked the formula.

02 — The Problem

A Country That Needs Protein But Can't Consume It Conveniently.

India's protein supplement market is growing, but it's failing most Indians. The products that exist were designed for a different consumer — typically affluent gym-goers willing to buy 2kg tubs and tolerate digestive discomfort. For the vast majority of the country, the existing options simply don't work.

The Old World — Why It Fails
Inconvenient: Protein powders require preparation, shakers, and planning — incompatible with on-the-go lifestyles.
Digestive discomfort: Most protein sources cause bloating and gut issues, especially for lactose-sensitive consumers.
High ticket size: Protein powders carry a high upfront cost and commitment, pricing out a large segment of Indian consumers.
Misconceptions: Protein is associated with bodybuilders and steroids — limiting mass market adoption.
The Be Brawn Way — What Changes
Grab-and-go format: A 330ml can you drink like any other beverage — at the office, gym, or on a commute.
Digestive enzymes: India's first protein beverage infused with digestive enzymes for superior protein absorption without discomfort.
₹120 MRP: Single-serve pricing with no long-term commitment — accessible to the everyday Indian consumer.
Natural & clean: Monk fruit sweetener, no artificial colours — making protein approachable for all demographics.
03 — The Product

Protein, Made Light. A Can That Changes the Category.

Be Brawn Protein Water is a 330ml ready-to-drink beverage built on Whey Protein Isolate (WPI) — the purest form of whey, delivering at least 90% protein by weight with minimal lactose and fat. Each can delivers 10g of clean protein at just 45 calories, sweetened with monk fruit, and enhanced with digestive enzymes for optimal absorption.

The product comes in four flavours — Lychee, Orange, Virgin Mojito, and Mango Punch — crafted to satisfy Indian taste preferences while remaining entirely functional. This is not a protein shake. It tastes like a refreshing fruit drink. That distinction is commercially critical.
